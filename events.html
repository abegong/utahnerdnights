How technically-savvy VCs think about startups

Our panelists are all venture capitalists who invest in technical startups. We will discuss the industry as they see it, the technologies they're excited about, advice for founders and prospective founders in technical industries, and field your questions.

Panelists:

  • Sarah Catanzaro, Amplify
  • Randall Lloyd, Grix VC
  • Bryan Offut, Index Ventures
  • Lee Edwards, Root Ventures (Moderator)

Food will be served. Masks and distancing will follow state and city guidelines.

  • 7:30 Panel Q&A

  • 9:00 Doors close
